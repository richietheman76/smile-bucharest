Dental Implants

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. We use premium Straumann, MIS, and Osstem implant systems — the same brands used in top UK clinics, at a fraction of the price.

Each implant is a titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone, topped with a custom porcelain crown. The result is a permanent, natural-looking tooth that lasts a lifetime with proper care.

  • ✓ Multiple implants & full arches
  • ✓ Same-day temporary crowns available

  • ✓ 5-year guarantee on implants
