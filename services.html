Restoration

👑 Crowns & Bridges

Dental crowns cap a damaged or weakened tooth, restoring its shape, strength and appearance. Bridges replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth. Both are crafted in our in-house lab for a perfect fit.

We offer zirconia crowns (the strongest, most natural-looking option) and e.max lithium disilicate crowns for anterior teeth. UK prices for a single crown start at £500–£900; our prices start at €80.

Smile Makeover

✨ Veneers & Hollywood Smile

Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front of your teeth. They can transform the colour, shape, size and length of your smile in just 2–3 appointments. The "Hollywood Smile" refers to a full set of veneers (typically 8–10 teeth) for a complete transformation.

Our dental ceramists craft each veneer individually to achieve a completely natural look. UK prices are £800–£1,200 per veneer; we charge from €180 — meaning a full Hollywood Smile set of 10 costs around €1,800 vs £8,000–£12,000 in the UK.

Full Arch Restoration

🦿 All-on-4 Implants

All-on-4 is a full arch restoration where a complete set of fixed teeth is supported by just four strategically placed implants. It's the most cost-effective solution for patients who have lost most or all of their teeth.

UK clinics charge £14,000–£20,000 per arch. We offer the same Straumann or Nobel Biocare protocol from €3,500 per arch — meaning a full mouth (both arches) from €7,000 vs £28,000–£40,000 in the UK.
